Become a member of the Drupal Association

 

The Drupal Association is an organization dedicated to helping the open-source Drupal CMS project flourish. We help the Drupal community with funding, infrastructure, education, promotion, distribution and online collaboration at Drupal.org. Funds to support these programs, and the Association staff come from memberships, supporting partners, sponsorships, donations, and volunteers.

 

A Drupal Association Membership (only EURO 22 or $30/year) funds community initiatives including maintaining Drupal.org and so much more.

 

Community Grants - The Drupal Association is accepting applications now. Imparting the importance of the Drupal Association in keeping Drupal.org upgraded and maintained. It's what we do!

 

There are over 800,000 Drupal.org profiles and just over than 3,000
Individual + Organisation members.

 

You can help to Drupal grow, by becoming a member or donating on Drupal Association.
