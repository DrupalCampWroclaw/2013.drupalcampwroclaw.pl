BECOME A SPONSOR OF DRUPALCAMP WROCŁAW 2013

We look for sponsors of DrupalCamp Wrocław conference, which gathers the best Polish specialists who use and implement the framework. If interested, visit Sponsors site – you will find there a PDF document with the sponsorship offer and the list of current sponsors. DrupalCamp Wrocław is organized on non-profit basis along with the Free and Open Source Software Foundation. The total amount of sponsorship payments will be devoted to the conference organization and the promotion of CMS Drupal in Poland.
 
Thank you!
